About The Trust

Zimbabwe against COVID-19

Purpose

The main purpose of the #ZimAgainstCovi-19 Trust is to provide Zimbabwean citizens (both at home and in the diaspora), corporates, and donors and well-wishers an efficient, inspiring, hope-filled vehicle for supporting initiatives aimed at preventing and fight against Covid-19 in Zimbabwe

Vision

To mitigate the loss of life and limit suffering on Zimbabweans during the outbreak of Covid-19.

Mission

To crowdsource financial, material and manpower support for the COVID19 response in Zimbabwe
